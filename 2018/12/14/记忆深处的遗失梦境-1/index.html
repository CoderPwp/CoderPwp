记忆深处的遗失梦境[1]

2018-12-14

黑暗是与生俱来的，而光明却是我们永生追求。

中文

在遥远的亿万年前，宇宙诞生之际。一切都是那么的虚无缥缈，黑暗疯狂的充斥每一个角落。群星寥落，星光黯淡。生命，在柔软的星云里安详的平稳呼吸着，伴随着宇宙微波背景辐射的美妙旋律挣扎，它渐渐睁开稚嫩的双眸凝望着这个新的世界。黑暗是与生俱来的，光明是我们永生所求。它望着远方的点点星光，尽情的幻想着光明的模样，唯有黑暗将它搂入怀中。抚摸，亲吻，保护它茁壮成长。

英文

In the distant billions of years ago, the universe was born. Everything is so illusory, darkness filled every corner. The stars are falling and the stars are bleak. Life, breathing peacefully in the soft nebula, with the cosmic microwave background radiation melodies struggle. It gradually opened his innocent eyes gazing at this new world. Darkness is innate, and light is what we eternal seeks. It looks at the stars in the distance, fell free imagine the look of light, and only the darkness puts it into the arms, stroking, kissing, and protecting it thrive.
